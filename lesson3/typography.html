Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display SC

Playfair Display SC is the chosen heading font as it captures the eye easily to direct focus on the headlines but at the same time, it is simple and clean.

Paragraph Font: Questrial

Questrial is the chosen paragraph font because of it simplicity and cleanliness. Since this is a chamber of commerce website, the goal is to inform and invite others to join and not to show off crazy fonts and designs. Thus, I should keep the fonts simple to keep the audience from being distracted.
